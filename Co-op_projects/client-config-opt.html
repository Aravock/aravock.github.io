Client configuration options

Call forwarding options

To set up call forwarding:

  1. Click on the settings wheel and choose Tools > Options, then select Call Forwarding in the menu on the left.
  2. Choose one of the following options:
    • Forward my calls to lets your calls be forwarded to a new number or a contact. Select New Number or Contact, then choose the contact or type in the phone number that you want your calls to be forwarded to.
    • Simultaneously ring lets your calls ring at work and on another phone or person at the same time.Just like above, select New Number or Contact, then choose the contact or type in the phone number that you want your calls to be forwarded to.
  3. Under These settings will apply, select All the time, or During work hours. You can set your work hours in Outlook 2013.
  4. Click OK to save your changes.

Saving conversations

Letting your conversation history be saved:

  1. In the main Lync window, open the Options menu by clicking on the Settings wheel, then select Personal in the menu on the left.
  2. Verify that there is a checkmark in the Save IM conversations in my email Conversation History folder box.
  3. If the check box is there and selected, your conversation history is already being saved.

Finding your conversation history:

Now that your conversation history is being saved, click the conversations tab in the Lync main window. Your conversations are being shown with the most recent at the top. Phone calls are also shown in the Conversations list. You can also find a past conversation with someone by right-clicking the person's name in your contact list and choosing Find Previous Conversations.

Lync pictures

Change your picture:

  • Click your picture in the Lync main window.
  • In the My Picture options, under My Picture, click Edit or Remove Picture.
  • Click Browse and go to the picture you want to use. Any size or resolution is fine.
  • Click the file and the click Save.

    • Hide your picture:

    You can hide your picture in Lync 2013, displaying a blank representation to other users instead of your picture. In the Lync main window, click your picture, and then select Hide my picture. Click OK to save your changes.
